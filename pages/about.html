About Central Petroleum Maintenance

Your trusted partner in the petroleum maintenance industry.

Our Story

Founded in 1990, Central Petroleum Maintenance has been a leader in providing high-quality maintenance and repair services to the petroleum industry. With decades of experience, we’ve built a reputation for reliability, safety, and professionalism.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to offer 24-hour service that ensures safe, efficient, and reliable operations for our clients. We take pride in delivering excellence and exceeding customer expectations in every project we undertake.

  • 24/7 Availability: Around-the-clock service for emergency and scheduled maintenance.
  • Skilled Technicians: Certified and highly trained professionals.
  • Customer Focused: We prioritize customer satisfaction above all.
